Healthy Lifestyle Cities 2021

Unravelling the factors behind a healthy city

Which cities are healthiest? Which cities have enablers and detractors for helping their residents stay healthy? What are some of the metrics of healthy living? In this data story, we try to look into the different factors that contribute to the healthy living of residents in 44 different cities across a variety of countries. We also share some of the reasons why we think these metrics would contribute to healthy living for the residents, and how some of these metrics could relate to other metrics that also affect healthy living.
